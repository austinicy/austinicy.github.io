    By studying the health care workforce data from World Health Organisation, World Bank Data and http://www.worldlifeexpectancy.com/to discover the insight of world health care workforce state in around 194 countries.

  3. Disease State for Countries

    By studying the health care workforce data from World Health Organisation, World Bank Data and http://www.worldlifeexpectancy.com/ help you know the disease state of your country.
